What is the trailers node?

The trailers node is a selection on the main menu in Portal that allows you to choose between several different forms that are focused on trailers.

What's it used for?

As mentioned above, the trailers node is used to select items having to do with Trailers in the DM Portal. It is the starting place for creating and managing trailer assignments.

How to use it?

This is pretty self-explanatory. Simply click on the node named: '110 - Trailers' then choose the sub node that you're interested in.

Management

The Management node is a sub-category for the Trailers node. Sub nodes below the Management node consist of editors that provide data for the Trailer View.

Procedures

Adding A New Trailer:

Click the ‘Go to a new trailer’ button near the bottom of the form. (See the ‘Common Controls’ document for more information.) The fields in yellow must be entered. Choose Brand/Warehouse, enter trailer number, seal, SCAC code, BOL/Manifest identification, date trailer was shipped, date trailer is/was expected to arrive, In Yard date, load type, who signed for the trailer, trailer priority, trailer status, and any comments. If you need to continue entering information for the trailer (changing information, entering a shipment), then you need to refresh the trailer by clicking the ‘Refresh Form’ button in the upper left corner of the form.

Adding A Shipment To A Trailer:

Select the shipment number, or enter a new shipment number, in the ‘Shipment’ box. Click the ‘Add’ button next to the ‘Shipment’ box. Information on the shipment will be added to the form from PkMS. The shipment details of the ASN and PO will be filled out. If there are any comments to be carried over from ‘Early PO Comments’ entries, they should appear. Enter the manifest cases by PO, if available. Note that the form just says ‘Cases’. This should be the ‘manifest’ cases. PkMS cases are calculated when the cover sheet or RCS is printed. The ‘manifest’ cases are the actual number of boxes the vendor shipped. Update any trailer, shipment, or PO comments if required It is then recommended that you refresh the trailer form at this time.

Updating A Shipment (PkMS Information)

If there have been changes made to the shipment in PkMS, such as the addition of a PO, then the shipment and its details in DM Portal will need to be updated. Select the shipment from the ‘Shipment’ box, and click the ‘Update’ button.

Removing A Shipment

In case of accident, you can remove a shipment from a trailer by selecting the shipment you wish to remove, then clicking the ‘Remove’ button. Finding A Trailer By Search Criteria You may search for a trailer by trailer, seal, SCAC code, shipment, ASN, PO, ‘In Yard’ date, or BOL by entering the criteria in the ‘Find’ box, the field you are searching in, and clicking the ‘Results Window’ button. If there are multiple options, a pop-up window will display all matching trailers, asking you to choose one. If a trailer isn’t found or a trailer is not selected, the current trailer displayed on the form will notchange. You can clear the criteria to reset the search by clicking the ‘Clear’ button.
