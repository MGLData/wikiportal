What is the procedural errors form?

The procedural errors form in Portal is a form that allows you to log an entry for an associate that describes their particular procedural error for a given department.

What's it used for?

The purpose of this form is to document the date of procedural errors performed by a specifc associate within a given department.

How to use it?

Simply choose an associate from the associate list. Once selected, that associate's info will populate on the form. When you are certain you have the correct associate, you will choose the date, kronos brand, department, error description and comments. The entry date and entered by fields will auto populate.
